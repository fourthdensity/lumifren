About

Photography is not about the thing photographed. It is about how that thing looks photographed.

I'm Elias Vance, a visual storyteller based between Lisbon and London. My work sits at the intersection of documentary and fine art — seeking authenticity in fleeting moments and finding beauty in the overlooked. Whether shooting editorial campaigns or wandering through unknown streets, I'm drawn to light, texture, and the quiet poetry of everyday life.
